  • MySoroban Online Class Syllabus

    Mysoroban School provides high quality soroban education for students aged from 4 to 14 years. Students can make full use of the systematic soroban learning platform called MySoroban. The courses are open to every registered school/organization/institute/teacher/student.

    Every School/ Organization/Institute can avail all activities of administrative management control tolls online, and also have full access to control branches, batches, teachers and students. It can also be used to generate accounts and performance reports.

    MySoroban Offers various types of Classroom facilities to every school/ organization/institute/teacher to conduct abacus classes online.

    Access has been given to every school/organization/ instituteto use MySoroban's Syllabuses and facilitating Tools to teach and train the students.

    Every school/ organization/institute is permitted to upload and teach their own syllabus. Access permission is given to the facilitatingTools during online class.

    Teachers can teach and train the students using their own flipchart boards or black boards without using facilitating tools of MySoroban and it's syllabus.

    Every Teacher will have full access to conduct class online with less plans as per level, live share to students to do class works and review the class works done by the students, and also to set sums to do home work as per lesson taken at every class.

    Every Student has options to choose their teachers world wide, students will have full access to know about their schedule of class, lessons in the Course/Level, duration of hours of a class. To improve their skills in mental maths it is permitted to practice and homework online through web app and mobile apps.
